How to Change Shortcuts

Chrome does not allow extensions to change shortcuts directly due to security policy.
Click the button below to open the official Chrome shortcuts page (chrome://extensions/shortcuts).
Find BookView and set your preferred keys.

* Allowed combinations: Ctrl+Shift+Key or Alt+Key
* Ctrl+Alt combinations and single Ctrl+Key are not allowed by Chrome policy
